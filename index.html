A Legacy of Simplicity

Established in 2000, Brown Board was born out of a love for natural ingredients and age-old traditional cooking methods. We believe that true flavor comes from the earth, uncompromised and organic.

Our ambiance reflects our food: simple, minimalist, and aesthetic. Whether you are craving the fiery, rich spices of authentic Hyderabadi cuisine or the comforting, crisp textures of classic South Indian dishes, we serve it all with uncompromising quality and at a budget-friendly price.
